HotCoin Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 6, 2025

1. Information We Collect

HotCoin is a social media application. To provide our service, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your username, email address, phone number, and profile details (like profile picture and bio).
  • Content and Usage Data: Any image or video content you upload, posts you create, likes, comments, shares, and search queries.
  • Automatic Data: IP address, device type, operating system, and information about your interaction with the app (e.g., time spent, pages viewed).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and maintain the HotCoin app and provide the social sharing features.
  • To personalize content and advertisements for you.
  • To communicate with you about your account, security updates, and service changes.
  • To monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with the app to improve our service.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and protect the safety and security of our users and services.

3. Sharing Your Information

Your profile information and content are publicly visible to other HotCoin users by default. We may also share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics).
  • Legal Requirements: If required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Deletion Request

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data. For this purpose, please use our dedicated Account Deletion Request Form. Once submitted, we will take commercially reasonable steps to delete or de-identify your personal information.

5. Child Safety and Prohibited Content

At HotCoin, we are committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment for all users. We have a zero-tolerance policy for any form of Child Sexual Abuse or Exploitation (CSAE) or harmful behavior toward minors.

  • Uploading, sharing, or distributing sexual, suggestive, or exploitative content involving minors is strictly prohibited.
  • Accounts engaging in such activity will be permanently banned and reported to law enforcement.
  • HotCoin does not allow any sexualization of minors or harmful acts toward children in any form.
  • Our app is intended for users aged 13 and above.
  • We actively monitor and cooperate with legal authorities to ensure compliance with global child protection standards.
